Tayo sa Huling Buwan ng Taon (2019)

Five years after their break-up, ex-lovers turned friends Sam and Isa attempt to find their own selves in the realm of their respective romantic relationships. What they discover instead is pain and uncertainty as they come to terms with their feelings for each other and everyone around them.

About

"Tayo sa Huling Buwan ng Taon" (In the Last Month of the Year) is a 2019 Filipino romance-drama film directed by Nestor Abrogena. It is the sequel to the 2015 film "Ang Kuwento Nating Dalawa," continuing the emotional journey of its central characters, Sam and Isa. The film explores themes of nostalgia, self-discovery, and unresolved emotions, capturing the essence of modern romance.
